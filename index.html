Diminutive Beginnings

Richard Evans, born into this world as a mere morsel of a human, stood no taller than a garden gnome at birth. His diminutive stature was apparent to all, even the nurses in the maternity ward who could hardly believe their eyes. As a baby, he had to use a step stool to reach his mother's milk. Little did the world know, it was just the beginning of a small man with outsized dreams.
